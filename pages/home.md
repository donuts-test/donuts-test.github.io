## About Us
DONUTS CoLab is an interdisciplinary research group that works at the intersection of AI, data, society, and safety. We aim to address the ethical and societal implications of AI technologies while ensuring their safe and responsible development and deployment. With a diverse team of experts, DONUTS Colab collaborates with industry partners, policymakers, and academia to drive meaningful advancements in AI that benefit society as a whole.Â
